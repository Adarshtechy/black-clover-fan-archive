Magic Fundamentals

Mana & Magic Power

Mana is the source of all magical abilities in the world of Black Clover. Every mage possesses different amounts of mana, determining the strength, scale, and efficiency of their spells.

Mana

Natural magical energy that exists throughout the world and within every living being.

Magic Power

The amount of mana a mage can control and convert into powerful spells and techniques.

Royal Advantage

Royals and nobles generally possess larger mana reserves, allowing them to cast stronger magic naturally.

Mana Zone

An advanced technique that allows mages to manipulate surrounding natural mana to enhance their spells.

Magic Flow

Mana → Magic Power → Spells
